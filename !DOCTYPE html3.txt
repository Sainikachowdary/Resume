<h1 style="color:Yellow;">KERALA</h1>
<p>“Oh Kerala, you are pure magic.”</p>
<p>1.Kerala is a state on the Malabar Coast of India.</p>
<p>2.It is spread over an area of approximately 38,863 square kilometers.</p>
<p>3.It shares its borders with Karnataka, Tamil Nadu, and the Lakshadweep Sea.</p>
<p>4.Kerala is the 21st largest Indian state by area.</p>
<p>5.It has an approximate population of 33 million people according to the 2011 census.</p>
<p>6.Thiruvananthapuram is the capital of Kerala.</p>
<p>7.The official language of Kerala is Malayalam. It is also the most spoken language in Kerala.</p>
<p>8.Mr. Pinarayi Vijayan is the chief minister of Kerala.</p>
<p>9.Some famous places in Kerala are the Periyar wildlife sanctuary, Munnar, Thrissur, Kovalam, and Wayanad.</p>
<p>10.Mohiniyattam and kathakali are the famous classical dance forms of Kerala.</p>
